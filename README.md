# Cold-Chain-Business-App-Prototype
## AquaTrack Logistics – IoT-Powered Fish Transportation Monitoring

AquaTrack Logistics is a hackathon project that was developed in 2024 to address challenges in fish transportation supply chain in Uganda through digitalization and IoT technology. As part of a multidisciplinary team, I contributed as the Application Developer, building a mobile application using Android Studio and Flutter. The application streamlined inspection workflows, centralized transportation data, and provided real-time visibility into cold-chain operations through IoT integration.

## Case Study / Problem Statement
Uganda's fish transportation supply chain is critical to the country's economy, food security, and export industry. Fish must travel through multiple stages, including harvesting, inspection, transportation, processing, and export to international markets.

However, the industry faces significant challenges. Poor road infrastructure, long transportation distances, and unreliable refrigeration increase the risk of spoilage. Inspection and documentation processes are manual, resulting in inefficiencies, data inaccuracies, and delays. In addition, the lack of centralized tracking and monitoring makes it difficult to maintain transparency and demonstrate compliance with international food safety standards.

Many existing logistics and cold-chain monitoring solutions are costly and inaccessible to small and medium-sized operators. Consequently, stakeholders struggle to minimize losses, maintain quality standards, and remain competitive in global markets.

## Solution
A low-cost, scalable digital platform that streamlines fish transportation through digital inspections processing and IoT-powered cold-chain monitoring. The application provides real-time visibility into shipment location and temperature, improving traceability, reducing spoilage, and supporting regulatory compliance.

## Key Features
* Digital inspection forms
* Real-time GPS tracking of fish shipments
* Temperature monitoring for cold-chain compliance
* Improved traceability and operational efficiency
